About Standard Room

The Standard Room is thoughtfully designed to provide guests with a comfortable and affordable stay. With simple yet elegant interiors, it creates a welcoming atmosphere perfect for both short visits and longer stays. Each room comes equipped with essential amenities including free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, a flat-screen TV, and a cozy work desk to balance relaxation with productivity. Guests can rest well on a queen-sized or twin bed, complemented by a wardrobe and daily housekeeping for added convenience. Room service is available, and complimentary bottled water is provided to ensure a refreshing experience throughout your stay.
