About Us

Welcome to Ricch Hotels – where elegance, comfort, and memorable experiences come together. located at H-No. 06-106/4/NR,Suchitra road,Quthbullapur, Hyderabad,Telangana, 500055 Ricch Hotels is more than just a place to stay – it’s a destination for relaxation, celebrations, and delightful dining. From elegant rooms & suites that blend modern comfort with warm hospitality, to banquet halls that serve as perfect venues for weddings, parties, and corporate events, we cater to every need with care. Guests can enjoy freshly brewed coffee, snacks, and multicuisine delicacies at our cafeteria & restaurant, or unwind by the Terrace Pond, a serene spot with beautiful views. To elevate your evenings, our live music nights offer soulful performances in a vibrant atmosphere. At Ricch Hotels, we believe in creating experiences, not just stays—whether you’re here for business, leisure, or a special occasion, we ensure your stay with us is truly unforgettable.
